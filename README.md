

Tools & Technologies Used
- Data Cleaning: Excel + MySQL Workbench
- Visualization & Dashboard: Tableau Public
- Data Source**: Superstore Dataset (Kaggle)

 What I Built
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Avg Discount
-  Profit by region 
- Sales by Category
- Sales trend
- Top 10 products 
- Interactive Dashboard with filters

This dashboard tells a clear story about the Superstore’s performance:

-Technology is the star performer — it leads in both Sales and Profit, making it the most valuable category for the business.
-Furniture shows high sales volume but weak profitability — indicating possible issues with pricing, costs, or product mix.
-Strong regional imbalance — West and East regions contribute the majority of revenue and profit, while Central and South regions underperform.
-Discounts are hurting profitability — Higher discounts often lead to lower (or negative) profit, suggesting the need for a more data-driven discount strategy.

 Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, here are my key recommendations for the Superstore:

1. Focus on Technology Category – Continue investing in this high-profit segment as it drives the majority of profitability.

2. Review Furniture Strategy – Investigate ways to improve profit margins (better pricing, cost control, or product assortment).

3. Optimize Discount Strategy – Reduce deep discounts on low-margin products, as they appear to negatively impact overall profit.

4. Regional Focus – Allocate more marketing and inventory resources to the high-performing West and East regions while investigating underperformance in Central and South.
